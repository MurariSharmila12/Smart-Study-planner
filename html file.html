Smart Study Planner

Organize study tasks, schedule sessions, and track your progress for smarter learning.

    Smart Study Tips

    • Use the Pomodoro technique: 25 minutes study, 5 minutes break.
    • Review tasks at the end of each week and adjust your plan.
    • Prioritize tasks by importance and deadlines.
    • Reward yourself for completing tasks to stay motivated.
    • Keep your study area organized and distraction-free.
